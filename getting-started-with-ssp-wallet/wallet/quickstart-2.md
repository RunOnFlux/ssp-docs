# Restore Wallet Using Seed Phrase

{% hint style="info" %}
Restoring Seed Phrase is a way to restore your wallet using your backed up wallet seed when you created your wallet.
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="info" %}
Please back up this seed phrase offline and store it separately. It backs up **Key #1 only** — one of the two keys in your 2-of-2 wallet — so on its own it cannot move funds or restore access; your SSP Key holds Key #2, which has its own separate seed phrase. Back up **both** seeds separately: if a key's device **and** its seed phrase are both lost, the 2-of-2 can no longer sign and the funds are frozen. By clicking "Show Mnemonic Wallet Seed Phrase" you will be presented with your own unique seed phrase.
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="info" %}
Installing SSP Key is needed before you can create a wallet

* iOS: [https://apps.apple.com/us/app/ssp-key/id6463717332](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/ssp-key/id6463717332)&#x20;
* Android: [https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=io.runonflux.sspkey](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=io.runonflux.sspkey)

Guide in installing SSP Key [https://app.gitbook.com/o/JAOVHXmS0zOqrz6ohvwK/s/OLYjN2kelo3HWVPLgZVU/\~/changes/11/getting-started-with-ssp-key/install-ssp-key](../../getting-started-with-ssp-key/install-ssp-key.md)

Scan the QR code using your SSP Key and approve the synchronisation on the Key (slide to approve, then confirm with biometrics or password)

Alternatively, switch to the manual tab and copy the wallet's sync data into your SSP Key via **Manual Input**

After the sync completes, both devices show the same **6-word verification code**. Scan it with your SSP Key or compare the words — they must match before you continue. If they differ, stop and re-pair. See [Pairing Verification Code](../../getting-started-with-ssp-key/pairing-verification-code.md).
