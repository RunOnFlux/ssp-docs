# View Transactions

{% hint style="info" %}
Viewing your transactions enables you to verify current and previous transaction from your wallet accordingly.
{% endhint %}

Open the "Activity" tab from the bottom bar (the clock icon)

Alternatively, you can export your transactions — at the bottom of the list you will find the "Export Transactions" button.
