# Portfolio & Activity

Two of the bottom-bar tabs introduced in SSP Wallet v2 give you the big picture across everything you hold.

## Portfolio

Open the **Portfolio** tab (bottom bar) to see:

* **Total balance across every chain and wallet** — tokens included — converted to your fiat currency
* **24-hour change** for your whole portfolio
* **Allocation** at a glance — how your value is distributed across chains and assets
* **Per-wallet rows** — tapping a wallet switches the app to it (a second path to the same switcher destination as the identity pill)

Chains you haven't activated yet appear in the list and can be activated right from there — with SSP Key v2, activating one or more chains takes a **single approval** on your Key (see [Switch Chain](switch-chain.md)).

{% hint style="info" %}
Balances across chains are fetched concurrently and cached, so the Portfolio tab stays fast even with many chains active.
{% endhint %}

## Activity

Open the **Activity** tab (bottom bar) to see your transaction history — sent, received, and pending transactions with full details. Click any transaction for fees, confirmations, and a link to the chain's block explorer.

From the bottom of the list you can also **Export Transactions** to a CSV file — see [Export Transactions](transaction/export-transactions.md).

{% hint style="info" %}
Transactions pending SSP Key approval are marked accordingly — every send needs both of your devices to sign before it broadcasts.
{% endhint %}

## Privacy mode

If you don't want amounts visible, **tap your balance** on Home to blur every amount across the wallet — including Portfolio and Activity. Tap again to reveal them.
